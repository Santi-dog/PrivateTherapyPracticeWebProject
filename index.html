Our Mission

Improve People's Life

We know how difficult it is to deal with bad behaviors from our children, specially with this issue is bonded to authism. The objective of CAMBIA is to provide a temporal service, in which the bad behaviors are addressed correctly and parents learn how to implement ABA at home with no further sessions from us.

Why Choose CAMBIA?

Our Training Sessions

We focus in different techniques & environments that kids could have in their daily life. Our training focus on public environment places, training for parents, progress analysis, work at home & playing sessions.

Hangout in the Community

Our children may have sensory / social difficulties in the community (dentist, hairdresser, supermarket, etc.), CAMBIA develops goals and accompanies the family to improve development in it.

Parent Training

CAMBIA in addition to training parents in ABA, supervises and practices with parents during the implementation of the technique to ensure success in learning.

Consistency

The best way to change a behavior is repetition. Being an intensive service ensures more progress in less time.

Progress Analysis

The goals are observed weekly through graphs of data obtained, to ensure that we obtain progress.

Home Service

One of the great benefits of working at home is working on personal and independence goals (going to the bathroom, brushing teeth, getting dressed, cleaning the room, etc).

Games

An essential part of development is through play. Social skills such as sharing, taking turns, following rules and knowing how to communicate with other children their age are worked on.
